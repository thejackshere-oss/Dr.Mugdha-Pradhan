Expertise & Background

Dr. Mugdha is a qualified Plastic and Cosmetic Surgeon with over 8 years of specialized Plastic Surgical experience. She specializes in hand surgery, aesthetic surgery, maxillofacial & craniofacial surgery, and reconstructive surgery.

She is adept at performing skin grafts, flaps, ear & nose reconstruction, and is highly proficient in anti-aging solutions and scar treatments.
